*Laundry & Dry Cleaning to your door let us take the load off your hands*

Laundry Drop is laundry and dry cleaning service that delivers at the click of a mouse — so you can get back to doing what you really love. Clean clothes are a team effort. We’re dedicated to taking care of your laundry and dry-cleaning every step of the way.

How it works

Order a pick up

Schedule for one of our drivers to pickup your laundry or dry cleaning.

We collect your order with custom bags so your clothes are protected in style.

Your clothes are returned 48 hours later.

Drop off to one of our stores

Simply drop-off to one of our 17 stores located in the wider Wellington region.

Our friendly staff will make sure your laundry or drycleaning is processed.

Choose to Pick up from the store you dropped off from or one of our other stores locations.

CARING FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

The Hydrocarbon® process is far less aggressive than traditional dry cleaning chemicals. So it’s better for your clothing, better for the environment and it doesn’t cost any extra! Our plastic wrap is biodegradable as well. Feel the difference

You really can feel the difference with Hydrocarbon® Cleaning. This pure solvent is safe to use with all fabrics, including delicate items such as wedding dresses. Dirt and stains are gently cleaned away leaving your clothes wonderfully soft to touch.

Smell the difference

You can really smell the difference too! The natural solvent doesn’t chemically interact with the fabric, so there’s no unpleasant odour with the Hydrocarbon® process.
