Laundry & dry cleaning delivered straight to your doorstep

Laundry Drop removes the hassle from laundry and dry cleaning. We deliver straight to your doorstep at the click of a mouse. Every garment is cared for to the highest standard so you receive the very best dry cleaning services available.

How it works

Order a pick up

Schedule for one of our drivers to pick up your laundry or dry cleaning.

We will pick up your laundry or dry cleaning from your choosen location.

Your clothes are returned 48 hours later.

Drop off to one of our stores

Simply drop-off to one of our 18 stores located in the wider Wellington region.

Our friendly staff will process your laundry or dry cleaning.

Choose to pick up from the stores you dropped off from or any of our other locations.

CARING FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

Laundry Drop uses the best dry cleaning technology available. We use Hydrocarbon® cleaning because it is far less aggressive than traditional dry cleaning chemicals. It’s better for your clothing, better for the environment and it doesn’t cost any extra! Our plastic wrap is also biodegradable.

Feel the difference

It is safe to use with all fabrics, including delicate items such as wedding dresses. Dirt and stains are gently cleaned away leaving your clothes wonderfully soft to touch.

Smell the difference

The natural soaps do not chemically interact with the fabric, so there’s no unpleasant odour with the Hydrocarbon® process. Your clothes are left smelling fresh and clean.

Our highly experienced team understands the complexities of treating individual garments in the correct manner. Many of Wellington’s high end fashion and bridal stores recommend us to clean their garments.
