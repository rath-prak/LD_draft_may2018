Frequently asked questions

Have a question? Find the answer in our FAQ's. If you have any questions regarding your exisiting order please contact us at the relevant store.

Your first order
  • What should I have ready for the first driver collection?

    Place your dry cleaning or laundry in a bag ready to be collected. To ensure your order is processed correctly please write your name on a piece of paper and leave inside your laundry bag.

  • What regions and suburbs do you cover?

    We currently deliver to company/business/public sector addresses in Wellington CBD, Lower Hutt CBD, Porirua CBD & Kapiti CBD.

  • How long does the cleaning take?

    Laundry or dry cleaning will be returned 48 hours after pickup. Pick up and delivery is weekdays and office hours only. Orders placed before 12pm will be collected on the same day and orders placed after 12pm will be picked up the following weekday morning. If your garment requires stain removal, repairs or any special treatment cleaning may take longer.

  • Can I add extra items to my order when you collect?

    No, a new order would need to placed via our website.

  • What items should be laundered and which should be dry cleaned?

    We will determine whether an item will be laundered or dry cleaned. The majority of the time, clothing will be dry cleaned and household items will be laundered.

  • Can I order by email or phone?

    Orders for pick up can only be placed online. For any problems email us at orders@laundrydrop.co.nz

  • Where are my clothes cleaned?

    We have a number of cleaning facilities around Wellington.

  • What fabrics don't you clean?

    All garments must be inspected before answering this question. We will let you know if we are unable to clean any items.

Dry cleaning
  • What if my clothes are damaged?

    We will contact you directly and agree on an outcome that leaves both parties happy.

  • Can all stains be removed?

    Not all stains can be removed.

Wash, tumble dry & fold
  • Do I need to weigh my Wash, Tumble Dry & Fold bag?

  • Do you dry my clothes?

    Yes

Pickup and delivery
  • When can you collect my order?

    Once your order is processed, our helpful delivery drivers will pick up your order. Pick up and delivery is weekdays and office hours only. Orders placed before 12pm will be collected on the same day and orders placed after 12pm will be picked up the following week day morning. Laundry or dry cleaning will be returned 48 hours after pickup. If your garment requires stain removal, repairs or any special treatment cleaning may take longer.

  • Timeframe to clean - how long does it take?

    Laundry or dry cleaning will be returned 48 hours after pickup. Pick up and delivery is weekdays and office hours only. Orders placed before 12pm will be collected on the same day and orders placed after 12pm will be picked up the following week day morning. If your garment requires stain removal, repairs or any special treatment cleaning may take longer.

  • Can I give you back my hangers from a previous order?

    Yes, we reuse hangers.

  • I missed a scheduled collection/delivery, what do I do?

    Contact us via phone or email or re-order. Phone: (04) 384 6668 or Email: orders@laundrydrop.co.nz

  • Can I cancel my order?

    Yes, contact our head office at Mall Dry Cleaning on Tory Street. Phone: (04) 384 6668 or Email: orders@laundrydrop.co.nz

  • What are your customer care hours?

    You can contact us anytime between the hours of 8am and 6pm.

  • What if I have an urgent order?

    We offer a 2 day turnaround service for laundry and dry cleaning. If you have an urgent order please contact us before placing an order through the website. Phone: (04) 384 6668 or Email: info@laundrydrop.co.nz
