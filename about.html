About us

Laundry Drop

We pick pickup and deliver company/business/public sector addresses in Wellington CBD, Lower Hutt CBD, Porirua CBD & Kapiti CBD. Or customers can pick up and drop off at any of our 18 stores in the wider Wellington region.

The Mall Group

The Mall Group is backed by 30 years of history. It all began in 1988, with the purchase of the first Mall Dry Cleaners. Fuelled by a passion for service, the Mall Group grew organically with the addition of more businesses including Taylors and Willis Dry Cleaners. 30 years later, we have become the Wellington region’s leading dry cleaners with 18 stores proudly owned by the Mall Group.

From the beginning, we have never lost our focus on quality and customer service. Some of our staff have been specialising in dry cleaning for over 30 years so you can be confident in leaving your garment with us. We employ the latest dry cleaning and laundry techniques and training methods, ensuring you get the very best dry cleaning services available.

Proudly Wellington owned by the Mall Dry Cleaning Group
